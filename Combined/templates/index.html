ABOUT

Kanvass is an innovative presentation service that allows you to amalgamate traditional presentation structures with new media applications on a single platform.


You can create new presentations from our catalogue of templates or design from scratch to a style that fits your needs.


The platform will give you the ability to add traditional presentation slides along with graphical analysis, media content, social media attributes, research studies and even location services which will provide the audience with a complete understanding of your creation.


You can design the structure of the presentation by chopping and changing their presentation until they are happy to share it with friends or colleagues.
